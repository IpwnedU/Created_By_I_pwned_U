My Mii was first made on my Wii in September 2008. I transferred it to my 3DS in 2011 and gave it a new nose. In 2017, I linked it to my Nintendo Account and adjusted the eye color and glasses color with the new color choices that were introduced for the Switch and the official online [Nintendo Account Mii Studio](https://accounts.nintendo.com/mii_studio). In 2025, I imported it into mii.nxw.pw to make custom renders.



All of my renders were made with the Miitomo (Default) Shader Type and the Mii Creator (Default) Editor Limit.



After configuring the settings, go to Create Mii > Mii data file > Choose Files and select I pwned U!.miic.
