Game Controller.png was exported in Inkscape from Game Controller.svg. Game Controller.svg is an edit of [the Twitter game controller emoji](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/twitter/twemoji/refs/heads/master/assets/svg/1f3ae.svg) from https://github.com/twitter/twemoji/blob/master/assets/svg/1f3ae.svg. I made the buttons on the right side match the coloration of those same buttons on a SNES controller, and I darkened the rest of the controller to make it look more like a Switch Pro Controller. I also went to Edit > Resize Page to Selection in Inkscape to perfectly crop the image.

ROFL.png was exported in Inkscape from ROFL.svg. ROFL.svg is an edit of [the Twitter ROFL emoji](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/twitter/twemoji/refs/heads/master/assets/svg/1f923.svg) from https://github.com/twitter/twemoji/blob/master/assets/svg/1f923.svg. I made the skin tone match my Mii, and I made the mouth and squinted eyes black. I also adjusted the color of the tears slightly and made them somewhat transparent.
